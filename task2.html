Angelina Jolie

Angelina Jolie (born 1975) is an American actress, filmmaker, and humanitarian. She is known for her roles in movies such as Tomb Raider, Maleficent, and Girl, Interrupted (for which she won an Oscar). Today she works not only as an actress but also as a director and UNHCR Goodwill Ambassador.

Important Events

  1. 1999 – Won Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress
  2. 2001 – Became UNHCR Goodwill Ambassador
  3. 2014 – Directed the film Unbroken
  4. 2020s – Focus on humanitarian work and directing

Famous Movies

  • Tomb Raider
  • Maleficent
  • Girl, Interrupted
  • Mr. & Mrs. Smith
